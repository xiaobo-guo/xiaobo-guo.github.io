I'm a 5th year PhD student in Computer Science working under the supervision of Soroush Vosoughi at Dartmouth College on Deep Learning. During my PhD study, I have been applied scientist interns at Amazon (2022, 2023).

Research

My work focused on computational social science, which is to apply deep learning methods, especially large-scale language models (LLMs), to social media for understanding people's opinions and beliefs. More specifically, I have first-author works on:

  • Multi-modal identification [ICPR] and dataset [ICWSM] of state-sponsored propaganda on social media

  • Media bias identification based on masked language modeling [ICWSM, EMNLP]

  • Early-stage mental disorders identification based on social media record. [WI-IAT]

  • Length bias elimination of summarization metrics. [EMNLP]
